about us

3 Threes Coffee Lounge, speciality coffee shops located in Birmingham, one being in the heart of city life and the other in the historic area of Jewellery Quarter. Both coffee shops serve gorgeous artisan coffee, whole leaf teas with handmade vegan and vegetarian foods, cakes and desserts to accompany. We offer things like sandwiches, wraps, paninis, bagels and our best seller, hot dogs.

Yes, coffee, hot dog and cake. Can you think of anything better?!
